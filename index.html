About me





Shashank Ahire has been working as a Research Associate at Industrial Design Center(IDC), IIT Bombay from past three and half years. He has completed his Bachelors in Computer Engineering in the year 2013. He is enthusiastically working in the field of HCI4D, and his area of research includes Emerging/Futuristic Technologies, Accessibility, and Text-input in an Indian language. While working in this area, in collaboration, he has published nine research papers at various international conferences like CHI, MobileHCI, INTERACT, and IndiaHCI. His work in the area of security and privacy for mobile has won Honorable mention award at CHI and Best paper award at INTERACT in the area of accessibility. Furthermore, His work has also been featured in the magazine such as Interactions. Presently, he is working under the guidance of Professor Anirudha Joshi from IDC at IIT Bombay, Dr. Charudatta Jadhav Head of Accessibility at TCS, and Professor Matt Jones. Head of Science at Swansea University. In his leisure time, he is involved in activities like sports, meditation, and reading.
